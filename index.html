CHAINSAWMAN

DENJI

AS

As a young boy, he inherits his father's debts from the Yakuza. After meeting Pochita, he becomes a Devil Hunter for the Yakuza in an attempt to clear his debt.
Once the Yakuza, under the control of a Devil, betrays him, he dies and Pochita becomes his heart. He is then revived and can become the Chainsaw Man by pulling a cord on his chest.
After meeting Makima, he became a Public Safety Devil Hunter as a member of Tokyo Special Division 4. Following Makima's death by his own hands, he resigned from his position as a Devil Hunter but continues to fight as Chainsaw Man under the public eye, all the while being enrolled in Fourth East High School as a student.
He is also the caretaker and adoptive brother of Makima's reincarnation, Nayuta.

ZYD

MAKIMA

Makima is the main antagonist of the Public Safety Saga. She was the Chief Cabinet Secretary's Personal Devil Hunter who took Denji in as her human pet. She is later revealed to be the Control Devil who embodies the fear of control or conquest and a member of the Four Horsemen at the time. Following her death, she was reincarnated as Nayuta.
